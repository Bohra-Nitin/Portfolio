AI-Powered Discovery & Needs Analysis Automation

An AI-powered instructional design automation workflow that transforms stakeholder meeting notes and learner insights into structured discovery reports, skill gap analysis, learner personas, intervention recommendations, and learning strategy outputs using n8n, Google Workspace, and Agentic AI workflows.

Automation Overview

This project automates the discovery and needs analysis phase of instructional design using n8n, Google Drive, Google Docs, and free LLMs. The workflow analyzes stakeholder meeting notes and optional survey reports to generate structured business-aligned learning insights, intervention recommendations, learner personas, and strategic learning outputs.

Primary Users

Instructional Designers, Corporate L&D Teams, Learning Experience Designers, Training Consultants

Core Technologies

n8n, Google Drive API, Google Docs API, OpenRouter LLMs, Agentic AI Workflows, HTML5, Tailwind CSS

Business Problem

Organizations often spend significant time manually analyzing stakeholder discussions, extracting performance gaps, identifying training priorities, and determining appropriate learning interventions. This process is inconsistent, time-consuming, and highly dependent on expert instructional designers.

  • Meeting notes are unstructured and difficult to synthesize.
  • Training requests are often symptoms rather than root causes.
  • Stakeholder expectations and learner realities frequently differ.
  • Manual discovery analysis slows learning project initiation.
  • Organizations struggle to identify whether training is even the correct intervention.

AI Analysis Engine

Discovery Analysis

Generates problem statements, business impact analysis, learning objectives, and skill gap analysis from stakeholder discussions.

Learning Strategy Recommendation

Identifies whether eLearning, blended learning, workshops, job aids, coaching, or non-training interventions are most appropriate.

Persona & Intervention Mapping

Automatically creates learner personas, intervention strategies, and content distribution recommendations.

Key Design Decisions

  • Workflow designed to function even when survey data is unavailable.
  • Structured prompts enforce consultant-level analytical outputs.
  • Google Drive trigger architecture minimizes manual workflow initiation.
  • Outputs optimized for business stakeholders and instructional designers.
  • Workflow separates training problems from non-training performance issues.

Generated AI Discovery Report

The automation generates a structured consultant-style discovery and needs analysis report directly in Google Docs. The report includes business impact analysis, skill gap identification, learner personas, intervention recommendations, and learning strategy guidance derived from stakeholder meeting notes and optional learner survey inputs.
